This addon adds an origin, power/action/condition types and various changes to bees.

## 🐝 Bee changes 🐝
- Bees now render equipped helmets.
- Bees now render their mainhand item in their stinger.
- Beehives now store the bee's armor, held items, and drop chances.
- Bees don't lose their stingers when holding an item in their mainhand.

## 🍯 Hivenest 🍯
A beehive that grew legs. It cooperates with bees to maintain itself and protects them.

## ✨Powerlist✨

<details>
<summary>Bee Nest</summary>

You can pick up and store up to 6 bees. You start with two. Picking up bees restores their health at the cost of honey if below half health.

Right click a bee to store it, press Primary to release the last bee that was stored.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Apicultured</summary>
  
Picking up bees with nectar will store it inside you. Overtime, nectar will be transformed into honey.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Mellivore</summary>

Food doesn't give any nutrients. You passively eat the honey you produce.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Bee My Friend</summary>

Bees won't get angry at you and will protect you.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Hive Armory</summary>

You can equip bees with weapons and armor. A bee with a weapon won't lose their stinger when attacking. Use shears to take your equipment back.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Slippery Hold</summary>

You deal really low damage with any melee weapon.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Swarm Recall</summary>

Call your bees towards your location. Recalling while sneaking will also store the bees, as many as you can fit. Bees released while sneaking will be instructed to not answer your calls.

Use by pressing Secondary.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Waggle Interpreter</summary>

You can tell when bees have found a flower and when they are low on health.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Beelocation</summary>

Interact with a beehive while sneaking to force the bees out.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Sticky Situation</summary>
  
You can phase through and climb honey blocks.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Expert Pollinator</summary>
  
Picking up a bee that has grown a crop will duplicate the flower in your offhand.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Sweet Nectar</summary>

Giving a bee flowers while sneaking will make them hold them. When the bee gets hit, they will consume the flower to gain potion effects.

</details>

## Modpack Development

<details>
<summary>Tags</summary>

- `blocks`
  - `flowers`: Blocks that make bees glow yellow.
  - `honey`: Blocks that can be climbed and phased through.
  - `campfires`: Blocks that give blindness when on top of.
- `items`
  - `flowers`: Items that can be held and consumed to gain effects by bees.
  - `honey`: Items that fill the honey bar when drunk/eaten.

</details>
